# HW2: AI-Assisted User Research Synthesis

## Workflow
Summarizing user research synthesis into actionable insights to share with leadership.

## User
A product manager, UX researcher, or strategist who regularly conducts user interviews, surveys, or usability studies and needs to communicate findings clearly and quickly to executive stakeholders.

## Input
Raw user research data — such as interview transcripts, survey responses, or session notes — along with optional context about the research objectives and target audience for the summary.

## Output
A concise, structured executive summary that highlights key themes, user pain points, and prioritized actionable recommendations suitable for a leadership audience.

## Why Automate This
Synthesizing qualitative research is time-consuming and prone to inconsistency. Researchers often spend hours distilling interviews into reports, leaving less time for actual research. Automating this step ensures faster turnaround, consistent formatting, and removes the cognitive load of translating raw data into leadership-ready language — making it easier to act on insights before they go stale.
